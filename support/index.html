Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and enter your email. You'll receive a password reset link within a few minutes.

Where can I find my API key?

Log into the Developer Portal and navigate to your Profile > Subscriptions to view your API keys.

How often is dispensary data updated?

Menu data is refreshed every 2 hours during dispensary operating hours (8am-10pm EST). Check status.terprint.com for real-time pipeline status.

What dispensaries are supported?

We currently support Trulieve, Curaleaf, MÜV, The Flowery, and Cookies Florida. More dispensaries are being added regularly.
