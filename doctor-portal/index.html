Terprint Doctor Portal

Comprehensive tools for MMJ physicians to manage patients, plan treatments, and access real-time cannabis product intelligence.

Overview

The Terprint Doctor Portal is designed specifically for medical marijuana physicians in Florida. It provides a comprehensive suite of tools to help physicians:

  • Access real-time dispensary inventory and product information
  • View detailed terpene and cannabinoid profiles for all Florida MMJ products
  • Make data-driven treatment recommendations based on patient conditions
  • Stay compliant with OMMU regulations
  • Track patient outcomes and adjust treatment plans

Registration

Requirements

  • Active Florida medical license
  • OMMU physician registration number
  • DEA number (if applicable)
  • Practice NPI number

Registration Process

  1. Visit doctor.terprint.com and click "Register"
  2. Complete the physician verification form
  3. Upload required documentation
  4. Await verification (typically 24-48 hours)
  5. Receive confirmation email with login credentials

Authentication

The Doctor Portal uses Microsoft Entra ID (Azure AD) for secure authentication. This provides:

  • Single sign-on (SSO) capability
  • Multi-factor authentication (MFA)
  • HIPAA-compliant session management
  • Automatic session timeout for security

Patient Management

Note: Patient data is stored in compliance with HIPAA regulations and Florida state law.

The patient management module allows physicians to:

  • View patient treatment history
  • Track consumption patterns and product preferences
  • Document treatment outcomes
  • Generate compliance reports

Treatment Planning

The AI-powered treatment planning tools help physicians make informed recommendations:

Condition-Based Recommendations

Enter patient conditions and symptoms to receive AI-generated product recommendations based on:

  • Terpene profiles optimized for specific conditions
  • THC/CBD ratios appropriate for patient tolerance
  • Product formats (flower, concentrate, edible, topical)
  • Current dispensary availability

Product Insights

Access comprehensive data on all Florida MMJ products:

  • COA Data: Certificate of Analysis data for every batch
  • Terpene Profiles: Detailed breakdown of dominant terpenes
  • Cannabinoid Content: THC, CBD, CBG, CBN percentages
  • Price Comparison: Compare pricing across dispensaries
  • Availability: Real-time stock levels at nearby locations

OMMU Compliance

Stay compliant with Florida's Office of Medical Marijuana Use regulations:

  • Automatic tracking of patient milligram limits
  • Route-of-administration compliance monitoring
  • Certification renewal reminders
  • Audit-ready reporting tools
